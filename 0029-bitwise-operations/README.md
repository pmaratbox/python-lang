# 0029 — Bitwise Operations

Compute bitwise AND, OR, and XOR on `6` and `3`, plus a left shift of `6` by one bit, printing `and: 2`, `or: 7`, `xor: 5`, and `shift: 12`. Python's bitwise operators `&`, `|`, `^`, and `<<` work on its arbitrary-precision `int`, so left shifts never overflow — `6 << 1` simply doubles to 12. `~` (NOT) and `>>` round out the set.

## Run

    python3 main.py
